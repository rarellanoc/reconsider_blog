About the Author

Former programmer and author of 'The Fragments of the Game.'

Rick became aware of web technologies as a creative medium in 2008. After formal studies in programming, he was fully immersed in research teams, helping bootstrap companies and applications from the ground up.

His current written work is characterized by a drive for social themes, mainly taken from the internet subcultures around man-woman dynamics. His most recent work is centered on authenticity, aided by previous theories and input from the art world.
